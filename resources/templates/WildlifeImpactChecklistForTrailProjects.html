WILDLIFE IMPACT CHECKLIST FOR TRAIL PROJECTS

INTRODUCTION

This comprehensive checklist helps trail planners and builders identify, assess, and mitigate potential impacts on wildlife and their habitats. Thoughtful planning that considers wildlife needs can create trails that provide recreation opportunities while protecting ecological values.

HOW TO USE THIS CHECKLIST

  1. Complete this assessment during the early planning stages of your trail project
  2. Consult with wildlife biologists or natural resource professionals when possible
  3. Document your findings and mitigation strategies
  4. Incorporate wildlife considerations into your trail design and management plans
  5. Monitor wildlife impacts after trail construction and adapt management as needed
